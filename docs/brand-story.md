# JCO Candles Brand Story

## Our Story

JCO Candles was born from a simple but meaningful passion: creating peaceful spaces through fragrance.

For the founder, candles have always represented more than decoration. They symbolize calm, harmony, comfort, and the feeling of coming home after a long day. Lighting a candle became a personal ritual—a way to slow down, relax, and create an atmosphere of serenity.

Inspired by a love for aromatherapy and home wellness, the founder began exploring the art of candle making. What started as curiosity soon became a passion. Through research, learning, and countless hours of practice, JCO Candles was created from the desire to share that same feeling of peace with others.

The name JCO holds a special meaning. It is inspired by the founder and her children, making the brand a reflection of family, love, and personal connection.

Today, JCO Candles continues to focus on creating high-quality soy wax blend candles designed to transform everyday spaces into relaxing sanctuaries.

Every fragrance is selected with intention.

Every candle is made with care.

Every product is created to bring warmth, balance, and tranquility into your home.

Our story

Created from a love for peace, fragrance, and home

JCO Candles was born from a simple but meaningful passion: creating peaceful spaces through fragrance.

For the founder, candles have always represented more than decoration. They symbolize calm, harmony, comfort, and the feeling of coming home after a long day.

Inspired by aromatherapy and home wellness, she began learning the art of candle making independently. What began as curiosity became a passion and a desire to share that same feeling of peace with others.

The name JCO carries a special family meaning, connecting the founder with her children and making every candle a reflection of love, care, and personal connection.
